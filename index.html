About Me


My name is PJ Almeida and I love to create. My passion for art, coupled with my background
in business and project management, lead me to web development.



Born on the tiny island of Guam, my life as a military brat has seen me travel the world
and experience culture through all walks of life. Now settled in Atlanta, GA, I am looking
for the next adventure! When I began my journey to learn programming, it wasn't about
changing careers, but moreso about adding an amazing new power to my skillset. I aspire to
use my experience and skills to passionately pursue projects that fulfill me.

In my spare time I enjoy learning new things, traveling, cooking and running obstable course races.
